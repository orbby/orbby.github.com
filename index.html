Rupeng Yang (杨如鹏)


I am currently a lecturer in University of Wollongong. Prior to joining UOW, I was a postdoc researcher in The University of Hong Kong, hosted by A/Prof. Man Ho Au. I received my PhD at Shandong University in 2019, advised by Prof. Qiuliang Xu.

I am interested in theoretical and applied cryptography, including:

  • Lattice-Based Cryptography
  • Cryptographic Watermarking
  • Privacy-Preserving Protocols
  • Public Key Encryption
  • Foundation of Cryptography
